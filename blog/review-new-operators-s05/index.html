Review: New Operators in Season 05

Posted on August 5, 2025 by COD Refill Team

Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Season 05 brings a fresh lineup of operators that add both style and tactical diversity to the game. Whether you’re dominating in Multiplayer, sneaking through Warzone encounters, or coordinating Zombies runs, these new characters have something unique to offer. Here’s our breakdown of their abilities, playstyles, and bundle details.

Operator Highlights

  • Raven – The ultimate shadow operative. Specializes in silent takedowns and enhanced sprint speed, making her ideal for flanking routes and close-quarters ambushes. Her bundle includes a dark, feathered outfit, a suppressed SMG blueprint, and a finishing move called “Midnight Silence.”
  • Blaze – A fiery force on the battlefield. Comes with incendiary perks that can deal burn damage over time, perfect for controlling choke points. Bundle perks include the “Inferno” LMG blueprint, flame tracer rounds, and a heatwave-themed calling card.
  • Viper – A tactical genius built for team coordination. Carries smoke deployment speed bonuses and recon drone boosts. Her bundle includes the “Venom Strike” AR blueprint, toxic green tracers, and a poisonous melee weapon skin.

Best Game Modes for Each Operator

Raven thrives in fast-paced Multiplayer maps like Nuketown and Firing Range. Blaze shines in Warzone’s urban zones where area control is key. Viper is perfect for objective-based modes such as Hardpoint or Domination, where her utility boosts the whole team.

How to Unlock

  1. Purchase COD Points from our discounted store.
  2. Go to the in-game Store and select the desired Operator Bundle.
  3. Confirm the purchase and equip your new Operator from the Loadout > Operators menu.

Pro Tips

  • Check for bundle sales during seasonal events—operators sometimes come with bonus weapon packs.
  • Pair Blaze’s perks with explosive killstreaks for maximum area damage.
  • Use Raven’s speed with SMG or shotgun loadouts for aggressive plays.
  • Viper’s recon bonuses make her a great fit for squads running UAV-heavy strategies.

Season 05’s operators aren’t just cosmetic—they can change the flow of your matches. Whether you want stealth, firepower, or tactical utility, there’s a perfect fit for your playstyle. Grab your favorite before the season ends to make sure you’re battle-ready.

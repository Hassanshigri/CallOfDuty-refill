2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze site performance, and provide personalized content. Cookies enable key features such as maintaining your cart, preserving login sessions, and delivering relevant promotions tailored to your interests.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize the following types of cookies to ensure a seamless user experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core website functionality, such as maintaining your cart and enabling secure checkout processes.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymized data on site usage to help us analyze and improve website performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Store your preferences, such as currency or language settings, to enhance your browsing experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver personalized advertisements and promotions based on your browsing behavior.

4. Managing Cookies

When you visit our site, a cookie consent popup allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may impact features like cart functionality or personalized recommendations.

We may use third-party services, such as analytics tools or advertising partners, which set their own cookies. These cookies are subject to the respective third-party privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them for more information.

Top Weapon Blueprints Worth Buying in Season 05

Posted on August 2, 2025 by COD Refill Team

Season 05 of Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 brings some of the best weapon blueprints we’ve seen yet. These ready-to-use builds don’t just look incredible—they’re tuned for peak performance in the current meta. Whether you’re holding down lanes in Ranked, dropping into Warzone, or speedrunning Zombies, these blueprints will keep you ahead of the competition.

Recommended Blueprints

  • Elite AR Blueprint – Built for laser-like recoil control and mid-range dominance. Perfect for aggressive pushes or holding angles in Multiplayer. Comes with a sleek matte camo and custom iron sights for faster target acquisition.
  • Neon SMG – High mobility, blistering fire rate, and neon tracer rounds make this a close-range monster. Great for flanking and clearing rooms in tight maps like Hijacked and Nuketown 24/7.
  • Sniper Master – Tuned for maximum bullet velocity and minimal sway, giving you one-shot potential from insane distances. A must-have for long sightline maps or precision Warzone plays.

Why Blueprints Are Worth It

Blueprints save you the grind by giving you pre-unlocked attachments, unique weapon skins, and often bonus effects like tracer rounds or dismemberment. They’re perfect for players who want a competitive loadout right out of the gate.

How to Get These Blueprints

  1. Purchase COD Points from the in-game Store or our discounted shop.
  2. Head to the Store tab in Black Ops 6 and check the Featured & Bundles sections.
  3. Select your blueprint bundle and confirm the purchase.
  4. Equip your blueprint from the Create-a-Class menu.

Pro Tips

  • Look for bundles that include both weapon blueprints and operator skins for better value.
  • Check seasonal events—some blueprints are free through challenges.
  • Experiment with blueprint attachments; you can still modify most loadouts to fit your playstyle.

Season 05 is loaded with high-performance gear. Don’t wait until these rotate out of the Store—lock in your favorite blueprints now and dominate the battlefield.

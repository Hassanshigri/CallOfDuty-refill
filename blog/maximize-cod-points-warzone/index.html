How to Maximize Your COD Points in Warzone

Posted on August 8, 2025 by COD Refill Team

In Call of Duty: Warzone, COD Points are your gateway to premium content—operator skins, weapon blueprints, finishing moves, and exclusive bundles. But with so many tempting items in the store, it’s easy to burn through your points quickly. Here’s how to spend smart, get more for your money, and keep your loadouts fresh without going broke.

Best Strategies

  • Invest in the Battle Pass – The Battle Pass is one of the best value purchases in Warzone. For 1,100 COD Points, you can unlock up to 100 tiers of rewards and even earn 1,300 COD Points back by completing it—effectively making your next pass free.
  • Wait for Bundle Sales – Keep an eye on seasonal or holiday discounts. Operator bundles can be 10–20% cheaper during special events, and often include bonus weapon blueprints, charms, and calling cards.
  • Prioritize High-Impact Blueprints – Some blueprints come with meta-ready attachments that can save you weeks of grinding. If a blueprint covers your main weapon in Ranked or Warzone, it’s worth the investment.

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Buying cosmetic-only bundles if you’re low on COD Points—focus on functional items first.
  • Purchasing every new skin on release—wait to see if it fits your playstyle before spending.
  • Ignoring free unlock events—sometimes the best cosmetics are available for zero COD Points.

Pro Tips for Saving COD Points

  • Stack your purchases during Double XP and Double COD Point weekends to get extra value.
  • Complete all daily and weekly challenges—many reward COD Points directly.
  • Check the store refresh schedule so you don’t miss limited-time offers.
  • If you play multiple COD titles, remember your COD Points carry over between them.
